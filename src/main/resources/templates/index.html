Bird Watching

Birdwatching is the recreational observation and study of birds. This activity gets millions of people around the world out of doors enjoying wildlife. Like hunting and fishing, birdwatching offers the thrill of pursuing and tracking wild animals, but does so in a much less intrusive way. Requiring little more than a pair of binoculars and a field guide, birdwatching is possible anywhere, with birds living on all seven continents.
